User

You can register as Student or Instructor. Students can take courses and Instructors can give courses

Taking Courses

Students can take courses as long as there is no time conflict. If so, a banner notice will be given.

Quit Courses

Students can quit courses they have already enrolled in.

Create Courses

Instructor can crate courses as long as there is no time conflict. If so, a banner notice will be given. Instructor can see who have already take their course

Course share

Users can share their courses to Twitter.

Letter sending

Users can send letter to another user. they can also see letter they sent and received.
